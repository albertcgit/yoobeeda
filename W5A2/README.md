# Retail Sales Dashboard

## Overview

This report use Power BI to show retail sales data. The data is from the Retail Sales Sample Dataset. It help us understand which product, region, and time period have the most sales.

---

## Data Cleaning

Before making the visuals, the data is reviewed and cleaned. No cleaning done as data is fine.

---

## Visuals

### Visual 1 – Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
This chart show the total sales amount for each product category. We can see that **Clothing** have the highest sales, follow by **Electronics** and **Furniture**.

### Visual 2 – Sales Over Time (Line Chart)
This chart show how sales change every month. Sales go down in **February** but rise again in **March**. This help us see the sales trend over time.

### Visual 3 – Sales by Region (Donut Chart)
This chart show the sales in each region. **West** region have the most sales at 32.1%, follow by **South** at 30.74%, **North** at 17.76%, and **East** at 19.4%.

### Visual 4 – Most Sold Item (Bar Chart)
This chart show which product have the most quantity sold. **Phone** and **Laptop** are the top selling products. **Desk** have the lowest quantity sold.
